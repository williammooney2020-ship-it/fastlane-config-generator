About Gemfile

Place the Gemfile in the root of your project (same level as your .xcodeproj). Run bundle install once, then prefix all fastlane commands with bundle exec to ensure the correct gem versions are used across your team and CI.


The generated Gemfile is a sensible starting point — uncomment the plugins you use.
